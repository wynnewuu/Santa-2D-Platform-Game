Control the player (pink capsule) by keyboard's up, down, left and right key. Press space for jump. 
Yellow cubes are collectable for player to collect. 
Red plateform indicate deathzone while blue platform is safe to land on and yellow plateform is safezone. 
If the player fall below the platform or touch the red plateform the game will restart.
Get to the end of the platform to finish the game. Once the player reach the last platform,
which is a semi-transparent blue platform then the platform will turn green to indicate the completion 
of the game. 
Have FUN!!!
